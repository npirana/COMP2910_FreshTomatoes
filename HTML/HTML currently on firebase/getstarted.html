Let's get started!

Fresh Tomatoes is a free, easy to use web app that will help to reduce your environmental impact and save you money. Once signed in you will have access to 3 different pages

Shopping List

The Shopping List allows you to make a shopping list before you visit the store. You can easily set the expiry date (in days) of perishable items by touching the blue button. As you buy items, you can check them off your list. Items can be removed from the list by touching the red delete button. Checked items will be added to your Fridge and can be safely deleted to clear your list.

My Fridge

My Fridge lists any items you've bought from the store. Items with expiry dates will appear in the top section, in the order they will expire. Non-perishable items will appear in the middle section. Expired and used items appear at the bottom in red. These items will be permanently deleted two days after they expired. If one of your items has expired early or been used, you can click the red trash icon to move it to mark it as expired/used.

Recipes

Recipes recommends a selection of popular recipes that will help to use up any soon-expiring ingredients.
